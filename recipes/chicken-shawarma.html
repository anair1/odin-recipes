Chicken Shawarma

Description

A simple Chicken Shawarma recipe that can be prepared at home by anyone with just a handful of everyday spices. Although typically stacked and sliced on a vertical rotisserie, we can cook ours horizontally on any old pan!

Ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Fresh lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Spices (Coriander, Cumin, Cardamom, Paprika, Cayenne, Salt, Pepper)
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken for 24 hours
  2. Preheat large non-stick skillet with 1 Tbsp Olive oil over medium high heat
  3. Cook chicken for 4-5 minutes on first side until nicely charred. Turn and cook the other side for 3-4 minutes
  4. Remove chicken from grill and cover loosely with foil. Set aside to rest for 5 minutes
